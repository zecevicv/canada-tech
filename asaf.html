ASAF TOUCHMAIRT

Entrepreneur at heart, Asaf Touchmair, was born with an unceasing passion for business and adventure. Starting his professional career as real estate agent, owing to an ironclad work ethic and vigorous determination he quickly skyrocketed to success as an independent enterpriser.

Armed with insight, hands-on experience, and know-how, Asaf set out with the goal of creating optimal profit for investors and allowing anyone to reach their maximum potential. Since day one, this vision has been Asaf's driving force behind every project, whether residential, commercial or leisure orientated.

Even today, as co-founder and owner of the renowned Canada-Israel Real Estate Group, Asaf insists on maintaining hands-on involvement, while paying meticulous attention to every detail and nourishing close relationships with each stakeholder, from clients to employees to contractors and architects.

Assaf is a pioneer investor in ProTech and ConTech companies. He was one of the first real estate developers that implemented technology into the Israeli real-estate sector. Among his investments: Daisy, Qbiq, Constru, Visitt, RST CleanTech, AuraSmart (TLV:AUSA), Reel (NASDAQ: REAX)
